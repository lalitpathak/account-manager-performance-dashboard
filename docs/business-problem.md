## Overview

Sales organizations often manage large customer portfolios, multiple pipelines, and regional account teams simultaneously. Without centralized analytics, monitoring performance and identifying business opportunities becomes difficult.

This project aims to solve that challenge by creating a unified Tableau dashboard for account manager performance analysis.

---

# Problem Statement

Business stakeholders need visibility into:

- account manager effectiveness
- pipeline growth
- customer retention
- opportunity conversion
- revenue contribution
- portfolio health

Traditional reporting methods make it difficult to:
- track KPIs efficiently
- compare manager performance
- monitor pipeline progression
- identify at-risk opportunities

---

# Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to provide:
- centralized performance monitoring
- pipeline intelligence
- customer account insights
- renewal tracking
- acquisition analysis
- executive-level reporting

---

# Key Business Questions

## Performance Analysis
- Which account managers perform best?
- Which managers drive the highest portfolio value?

## Pipeline Insights
- What is the current pipeline value?
- Which deal stages contain the largest opportunities?

## Customer Intelligence
- Which accounts contribute the most revenue?
- Which opportunities are likely to close successfully?

## Revenue Monitoring
- How is revenue evolving over time?
- What are the conversion trends?

---

# Business Impact

The dashboard helps organizations:
- improve sales visibility
- optimize pipeline management
- identify growth opportunities
- monitor customer retention
- support strategic planning
- improve executive reporting efficiency

---

# Conclusion

The Regional Account Manager Insights Dashboard transforms operational sales data into actionable business intelligence, enabling data-driven decision-making and performance optimization.
