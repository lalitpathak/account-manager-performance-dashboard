# KPI Definitions

This document defines the KPIs and analytical metrics used in the Regional Account Manager Insights Dashboard.

---

# 1. Performance Rank

## Definition
Overall ranking of the selected account manager compared to all managers.

## Business Importance
Measures comparative performance effectiveness.

---

# 2. Conversion Rate (%)

## Definition
Percentage of opportunities successfully converted into closed business.

## Formula
Conversion Rate = (Closed Won Opportunities / Total Opportunities) × 100

## Business Importance
Evaluates sales effectiveness and closing efficiency.

---

# 3. Portfolio Value (£)

## Definition
Total value of active customer accounts managed by an account manager.

## Business Importance
Measures customer portfolio strength.

---

# 4. Active Customers

## Definition
Total number of currently active customer accounts.

## Business Importance
Indicates customer engagement and account coverage.

---

# 5. Pipeline Value

## Definition
Total monetary value of all open opportunities.

## Business Importance
Helps assess future revenue potential.

---

# 6. Pipeline Growth

## Definition
Percentage increase or decrease in pipeline value compared to previous periods.

## Business Importance
Tracks business growth momentum.

---

# 7. Average Deal Size

## Definition
Average revenue value generated per closed opportunity.

## Formula
Average Deal Size = Total Revenue / Number of Closed Deals

## Business Importance
Measures revenue efficiency per opportunity.

---

# 8. Closed Won vs Closed Lost

## Definition
Comparison between successfully closed opportunities and lost opportunities.

## Business Importance
Helps evaluate sales effectiveness and opportunity quality.

---

# 9. Top Accounts

## Definition
Highest-value customer accounts managed by the selected account manager.

## Business Importance
Supports customer prioritization and relationship management.

---

# 10. New Acquisitions

## Definition
Newly acquired customer opportunities.

## Business Importance
Measures business expansion capability.

---

# 11. Lost Opportunities

## Definition
Opportunities that failed to convert into business.

## Business Importance
Identifies potential weaknesses in pipeline conversion.

---

# 12. Upcoming Renewals

## Definition
Customer contracts or opportunities approaching renewal dates.

## Business Importance
Supports customer retention planning and proactive engagement.

---

# Conclusion

The KPIs in this dashboard provide a comprehensive view of:
- sales performance
- pipeline health
- portfolio management
- customer retention
- revenue generation

Together, these metrics support strategic business decision-making and performance optimization.
